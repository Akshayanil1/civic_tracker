# Meta Ads & Social Media Strategy

## Target Audience
- **GovTech Leaders & Municipal Commissioners (India)**
- **Open-Source Contributors & Python/Frappe Developers**
- **Civic NGOs & Transparency Advocates**

## 1. High-Quality Poster Concept
**Visual:** A split-screen graphic. 
- *Left Side (Dark/Red):* A chaotic pile of paper forms and an overflowing garbage bin. Text: "The Old Way: Zero Accountability."
- *Right Side (Clean/Blue):* A sleek dashboard showing AI processing an image of a pothole, generating a financial penalty. Text: "The Civic SLA Engine: AI-Enforced Accountability."
**Call to Action:** "Star the Repo on GitHub"

## 2. 60-Second Video Script (For Reels/LinkedIn)

**(0:00-0:10)** *Hook:* "What if a pothole could fine the contractor who didn't fix it on time? I built an open-source platform that does exactly that."

**(0:10-0:25)** *The Problem:* "Indian municipalities struggle with accountability. So I built 'Civic Tracker' using the Frappe Framework. When a citizen reports a flooded street via our WhatsApp bot, the system starts a strict SLA countdown."

**(0:25-0:40)** *The Tech:* "To prevent spam, an AI Vision model verifies the image. If the contractor ignores it and the timer hits zero, a Redis-backed cron job automatically deducts a penalty straight from their ERPNext invoice."

**(0:40-0:60)** *Call to Action:* "It's fully multi-tenant, offline-first, and open-source. If you're passionate about GovTech, check out the architecture on my GitHub and drop a star!"
